# ICP Tier 2: India BIM Service Providers

**Employees:** 20–200  
**Model:** Provide BIM modeling, clash detection, VDC to global clients
**Decision maker:** Founder/CEO or VDC Manager

## Profile
- Commoditized modeling market — need differentiation
- Clients ask "did you check everything?" — can't prove it
- Competing on price with 20+ other firms
- Pain: Need value-add beyond 3D modeling

## Example Targets
- TechnoStruct Academy graduates ($16M valuation target, 81% growth)
- Excelize (Nagpur Metro BIM partner)
- Revit Modeling India
- TAAL Tech

## Why They Buy
1. **"We need to differentiate from cheap modeling shops"** — Document intelligence = premium service
2. **"Clients demand proof of checking"** — Audit trail = client retention
3. **"Our best people leave for firms with better tools"** — Talent retention

## Outreach Channel Priority
1. LinkedIn (BIM consultant networks)
2. Partnership with training institutes
3. Autodesk India partner events
4. Cold email to VDC Manager titles

## Pricing Sensitivity
- Per-project pricing preferred
- White-label opportunity (resell to their clients)
- Revenue share model possible
