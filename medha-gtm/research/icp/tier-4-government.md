# ICP Tier 4: Government / Public Sector Agencies

**Mandate:** BIM adoption required  
**Need:** Audit trails for compliance  
**Decision maker:** Chief Digital Officer, Head of Technical Standards

## Profile
- Mandated BIM adoption but limited execution capability
- Need audit trails to prove due diligence
- Public scrutiny on project failures
- Pain: Proving compliance across thousands of documents

## Example Targets
- Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC)
- NHAI (National Highways)
- Singapore BCA (Building & Construction Authority)
- UK Infrastructure & Projects Authority

## Why They Buy
1. **"We need to prove we checked everything"** — Audit trail = political cover
2. **"Mandates require compliance we can't verify"** — Automated verification
3. **"One project failure = headlines + inquiry"** — Risk mitigation

## Outreach Channel Priority
1. RFP responses (public procurement)
2. Industry conferences (government track)
3. Partner with existing government contractors
4. Reference public audit findings (Palo Alto, etc.)

## Pricing Sensitivity
- Budget allocated but procurement-heavy
- Requires security certifications
- Pilot → multi-year contract path
- References from other agencies critical
