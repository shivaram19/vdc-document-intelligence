# ICP Tier 1: India Mid-Market General Contractors

**Revenue:** $10M–$100M  
**Employees:** 50–500  
**Active projects:** 3–15  
**Decision maker:** Project Director or Managing Director

## Profile
- Mix of commercial, residential, infrastructure
- Already "BIM-curious" but not BIM-native
- Documents scattered across WhatsApp, email, shared drives
- Pain: Scale is outpacing document management capability

## Example Targets
- Shapoorji Pallonji (infrastructure division)
- Gammon India
- Mid-tier contractors in Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune tech corridors
- Smart City implementation partners

## Why They Buy
1. **"I can't afford a rework on my reputation"** — One public failure kills future bids
2. **"My PMs spend more time searching than managing"** — Inefficiency at scale
3. **"Smart Cities require digital compliance"** — Mandate-driven adoption

## Outreach Channel Priority
1. WhatsApp Business (India primary)
2. LinkedIn India
3. Industry events (ACE, IIIC partnerships)
4. IndiaMART / Sulekha listings

- UPI + Razorpay required
- GST invoicing mandatory
- "Pay after first project" pilot builds trust
