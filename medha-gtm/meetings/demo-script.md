# Demo Script
## 15-Minute Live Demo (5P Framework)

---

## Hook (0–30s)
> "You mentioned that [SPECIFIC PAIN] — like when [THEIR STORY]. I'm going to show you how Medha handles exactly that."

## Promise (30–60s)
> "In the next few minutes, I'll show you three things:
> 1. How to find any spec clause in under 10 seconds
> 2. How to catch contradictions before they become RFIs
> 3. How to draft an RFI that references source documents automatically
>
> And this is all on your actual project documents."

## Proof (60–120s) — Live Demo

**Demo 1: Instant Spec Lookup (45s)**
> "You said your PMs spend 20 minutes searching. Watch this."
> [Type their actual question]
> "Here's the answer, with the exact source document cited."

**Demo 2: Contradiction Scan (45s)**
> "You told me about the time the drawing said 6 inches and the spec said 8."
> [Run scan_contradictions]
> "Here — it flagged the structural spec calls for 5,000 psi but the drawing notes say 4,000."

**Demo 3: RFI Draft (30s)**
> "When you DO need to write an RFI — Medha drafts it with citations."
> [Type: "What is the concrete strength for column C-12?"]
> "Here's a draft RFI, already referencing the spec section and drawing sheet."

## Path (60s)
> "This isn't replacing your BIM model or PM tool. Medha sits alongside them. It reads your document set — PDFs, Word, scanned drawings — and makes them queryable. And every user has to prove project knowledge before access. Three-factor authentication."

## Push (CTA — 30s)
> "What I just showed you — would that save time on [THEIR PROJECT]?"

Wait for answer. If yes → move to commitment ladder.
