## Tactics

### Comment on VDC Job Posts
When a company posts "We're hiring a BIM Document Manager":  
*"Instead of hiring one person to read 10,000 pages, what if the documents could answer questions themselves? We're piloting this with 3 GCs right now."*

### LinkedIn Polls
*"How long does it take your team to find a spec clause during a concrete pour?"*
- A) Under 2 min
- B) 2–10 min
- C) 10–30 min
- D) We just guess

Everyone voting C or D = lead.
