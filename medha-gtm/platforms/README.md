# Platform Comparison Matrix

| Platform | Audience | Pain Visibility | Outreach Speed | Commitment Potential | Priority |
|----------|----------|-----------------|----------------|---------------------|----------|
| **Reddit** | GCs, PMs, supers | High (public complaints) | Fast (comments/DMs) | Medium | ★★★★★ |
| **LinkedIn** | VDC managers, directors | Medium (job posts = pain) | Medium (sequences) | High | ★★★★★ |
| **Discord** | BIM pros, students | High (real-time chat) | Fast (community) | Medium | ★★★★☆ |
| **YouTube** | BIM learners, practitioners | Medium (comments) | Slow (content) | Low | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Forums** | Engineers, GCs | High (technical depth) | Medium | Medium | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Glassdoor/Jobs** | Hiring managers | High (hiring = pain) | Fast (DM hiring mgr) | High | ★★★★☆ |
| **Government** | CDOs, standards heads | Low (formal) | Slow (RFPs) | Very High | ★★★★☆ |
| **India-specific** | SMB contractors | High (WhatsApp) | Fast (QR/viral) | Medium | ★★★★★ |
| **Software markets** | Software buyers | Medium (reviews) | Medium | Medium | ★★★☆☆ |
