# Glassdoor & Job Boards

## Intelligence From Job Postings
- "BIM Document Control" combined role = no tool, only people
- "Corporate BIM Director" $180K–$250K = enterprise budget, massive pain
- 3,838+ BIM jobs in US alone

## Tactics

### Apply to the Pain, Not the Job
Find companies hiring Document Controllers.  
LinkedIn DM the hiring manager:  
*"Saw you're hiring for document control. What if instead of hiring 2 people, you could give your existing team AI that reads every spec? We're piloting with 3 firms. Worth a 10-min chat?"*

### Track Hiring Velocity
Companies hiring 3+ BIM roles in 6 months = scaling fast = hot prospect.

## Metrics
- Hiring managers contacted: 10/week
- Meeting conversion: 15–20%
