## Tactics

### Respond to RFPs
When NHAI/DMRC issues BIM-related RFPs:  
Proposal section: "Automated Document Compliance Verification"

### Reference Public Audit Findings
*"The Palo Alto City Auditor found that poor document management prevents defending against claims. Medha's tamper-evident audit trail solves this."*

### Smart City Pilots
Approach implementation partners:  
*"Free pilot for one Smart City project. We'll index all documents and catch contradictions before construction starts."*
