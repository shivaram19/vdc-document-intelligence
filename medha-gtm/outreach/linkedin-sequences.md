# LinkedIn Sequences

## Sequence for Tier 1 India Mid-Market

```
Day 1:  Comment on prospect's post about project challenges
Day 3:  Comment again + share relevant industry stat
Day 5:  Connection request: "Saw your work on [project]. 
        Curious how you handle spec lookups on site?"
Day 8:  DM: "Quick question — do your PMs ever waste time 
        searching for clauses during pours? We built something 
        for that."
Day 14: Share case study (not pitch)
Day 21: Soft pitch: "Happy to show you how [similar firm] 
        cut RFI time by 60%"
```

## LinkedIn Content Calendar (Weekly)

- **Monday:** Industry stat + "What we see" observation
- **Wednesday:** 60-second video: "Here's a contradiction we found"
- **Friday:** Case study carousel: "Before/After: How [Client] eliminated duplicate RFIs"

## Connection Request Templates

**For VDC Managers:**
> "Hi [Name], saw your work on [Project] — impressive coordination. Curious: how do you handle spec lookups when the model doesn't have the answer? Would love to connect."

**For Project Directors:**
> "Hi [Name], [Company] is doing interesting work in [sector]. I'm researching how mid-market GCs handle document compliance at scale. Would value your perspective."

## DM Templates (After Connection)

**Value-First DM:**
> "Thanks for connecting, [Name]. I noticed [Company] is working on [project type]. We recently published a short analysis on document contradictions in [similar project]. Happy to share if it's relevant."

**Soft Pitch DM:**
> "[Name], quick question: on your [project type] projects, how much time does your team spend searching for spec clauses? We built something that cuts it to 10 seconds — worth a brief look?"
