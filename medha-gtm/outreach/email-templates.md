# Email Templates

## Template 1: Cold Email to BIM Service Providers

**Subject:** [Company] + spec lookup time on [project type]

```
Hi [First Name],

I saw [Company] delivered BIM coordination for [Project]. Impressive work.

Quick question: When your VDC team gets asked "what's the concrete strength
for Section C?" — how long does it take to find the answer?

Most teams we speak to say 15–45 minutes of document hunting.

We built Medha to cut that to <10 seconds — and catch contradictions
automatically before they become RFIs.

Worth a 10-minute conversation?

[Name]
Medha — Document Intelligence for VDC Teams
```

## Template 2: Follow-Up After Discovery Call

**Subject:** [Project] + next steps

```
Hi [First Name],

Thanks for the conversation today. Two things stuck with me:

1. [Specific pain they mentioned]
2. [Specific story they told]

I promised to send you [specific thing]. Attached.

Also — would a 30-day pilot on [their active project] make sense?
We set it up, measure time saved, and decide together if it's worth continuing.

[Calendar link]

[Name]
```

## Template 3: Re-Engagement (Gone Cold)

**Subject:** [Their project type] + contradiction we found

```
Hi [First Name],

I was running Medha on a [their project type] document set and found
something that reminded me of our conversation:

[Specific contradiction — e.g., "The structural spec called for 5,000 psi
concrete. The drawing notes said 4,000."]

This is exactly the kind of thing that slips through on busy projects.

Still worth a 10-minute look at how this would work on your docs?

[Name]
```

## Benchmarks
- Reply rate target: 3–5%
- Open rate target: >40%
- Sequence length: 4–6 touches over 14–21 days
