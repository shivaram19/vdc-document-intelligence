# Trayini.ai — VDC Document Intelligence
## Investor / Partner Pitch (One-Pager)

---

### The Problem
VDC agencies spend **15+ hours per project per week** manually reviewing construction drawings, specifications, RFIs, and submittals. This is billable time that scales linearly with headcount. There is **no AI tool built specifically for VDC agencies** — they are forced to use generic document management or enterprise GC software that they cannot white-label.

### The Product
**White-label AI document intelligence for VDC agencies.**

Upload a project's full document set. Ask questions in plain English. Get cited answers from the actual drawings and specs. Auto-draft RFI responses. Detect drawing-spec contradictions before they reach the field.

### The Market
- **AI in BIM market:** $462M (2025) → $968M (2032), CAGR 11.3%
- **VDC agency market:** 27+ agencies identified, $3K–$5K/mo typical pricing
- **Construction document management:** Procore ($1B+ rev), Pelles.ai ($1,999/yr), but **zero white-label solutions for VDC agencies**
