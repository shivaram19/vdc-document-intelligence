1. FIRE-RATED DOOR REVISIONS

   The following changes supersede Section 08 71 00 of the Specifications:

   1.1 Stairwell doors: REVISED from 90-minute to 120-minute fire rating (2-hour label).
       - All stairwell doors shall now meet 2-hour fire resistance rating per UL 10C.
       - This applies to all stairwell exit doors at Levels B through 20.
       - Existing orders for 90-minute doors must be revised.

   1.2 Corridor doors: REVISED from 45-minute to 90-minute fire rating (1.5-hour label).
       - All corridor doors shall now meet 90-minute rating.
       - This applies to all unit entry doors and corridor cross-over doors.
       - Frames must be upgraded to 14-gauge steel.

   1.3 Mechanical room doors: UNCHANGED at 90-minute rating.

   1.4 Electrical room doors: REVISED from 60-minute to 90-minute fire rating.
       - All electrical room doors require 90-minute rating with 14-gauge frames.

   1.5 Tenant separation doors: REVISED from 45-minute to 60-minute fire rating.
       - Demising walls between tenant spaces now require 1-hour rated doors.

2. SLAB THICKNESS CLARIFICATION

   2.1 The structural drawing S-101 indicates typical elevated slab at 6 inches.
   2.2 The structural specification Section 03 30 00 requires 8 inches minimum.
   2.3 CLARIFICATION: The 8-inch thickness per specification governs. Drawing S-101 shall be revised in Addendum 4 to show 8-inch slab.
   2.4 Contractor shall proceed with 8-inch slab pending formal drawing revision.

3. CONCRETE STRENGTH CLARIFICATION

   3.1 Column schedule on Drawing S-101 shows C-1 through C-12 at 5,000 psi.
   3.2 Structural specification Section 2.1 requires 6,000 psi for all columns.
   3.3 CLARIFICATION: All columns including C-1 through C-12 shall be 6,000 psi.
   3.4 Drawing S-101 shall be revised in Addendum 4. Contractor to proceed at 6,000 psi.

4. DUCTWORK CLEARANCE ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENT

   4.1 MEP specification requires 8-inch minimum clearance from structure.
   4.2 ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENT: Where ducts cross below beams, maintain 12-inch clearance to bottom of beam for inspection access.
   4.3 Coordinate with structural to confirm beam depths do not conflict.

5. SPRINKLER SPACING CLARIFICATION

   5.1 Fire protection spec Section 21 13 00 states maximum 15 feet on center.
   5.2 Architectural reflected ceiling plan A-302 shows sprinkler layout at 14 feet on center.
   5.3 CLARIFICATION: Architectural layout at 14 feet governs. Sprinkler contractor to coordinate with ceiling grid layout.
