PROJECT: HIGHRISE TOWER - PHASE 2
MECHANICAL SPECIFICATIONS
SECTION 23 00 00 - HEATING, VENTILATION, AND AIR CONDITIONING

1. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
   1.1 All HVAC work shall conform to ASHRAE 90.1-2019 and local mechanical code.
   1.2 All equipment shall be Energy Star rated where available.
   1.3 Contractor shall provide 1-year warranty on all equipment and workmanship.

2. DUCTWORK REQUIREMENTS
   2.1 Supply ductwork: galvanized steel, minimum 26 gauge for ducts up to 30 inches.
   2.2 Return ductwork: galvanized steel, minimum 24 gauge.
   2.3 Ductwork above 30 inches: reinforced with angle iron supports at 6 feet on center.
   2.4 All ductwork shall be internally lined with 1-inch fiberglass insulation where noted.
   2.5 Ductwork clearance to structure: 8 inches minimum from all beams and columns.
   2.6 Ductwork clearance to slab soffit: 6 inches minimum for maintenance access.
   2.7 Flexible ductwork: maximum 5 feet in length, UL 181 Class 1.

3. AIR HANDLING UNITS
   3.1 AHU-1 (Roof): 25,000 CFM capacity, variable frequency drive.
   3.2 AHU-2 through AHU-5 (Mechanical floors): 15,000 CFM each.
   3.3 All AHUs shall have MERV 13 filters minimum.
   3.4 Outside air intake: 15% minimum per ASHRAE 62.1.
   3.5 AHU casing: double-wall construction with 2-inch insulation.

4. DIFFUSERS AND GRILLES
   4.1 Ceiling diffusers: linear slot type, aluminum finish.
   4.2 Return grilles: perforated face, hinged for filter access.
   4.3 Diffuser spacing: maximum 10 feet on center in open areas.
   4.4 Perimeter zones: dedicated diffusers at 8 feet on center.

5. CONTROLS
   5.1 Building Automation System (BAS): BACnet protocol, IP-based.
   5.2 Zone temperature setpoint: 72 degrees Fahrenheit cooling, 70 degrees Fahrenheit heating.
   5.3 Occupancy sensors: auto- setback to 78 degrees Fahrenheit unoccupied.
   5.4 CO2 sensors required in all conference rooms and assembly areas.
   5.5 All control wiring in conduit, minimum 3/4 inch EMT.

6. INSULATION
   6.1 Ductwork insulation: 2-inch fiberglass, R-8 minimum.
   6.2 Pipe insulation: 1-inch fiberglass for chilled water, 1.5-inch for hot water.
   6.3 Insulation jacket: UV-resistant PVC for all exterior exposed piping.
   6.4 Fire-rated enclosures: 2-hour rating where penetrating rated assemblies.

---
SECTION 22 00 00 - PLUMBING

1. GENERAL
   1.1 All plumbing shall conform to IPC 2021 and local amendments.
   1.2 Water service: 6-inch domestic, 8-inch fire protection from city main.
   1.3 All fixtures shall be low-flow, WaterSense labeled.

2. PIPE MATERIALS
   2.1 Domestic water: Type L copper above ground, ductile iron below ground.
   2.2 Sanitary waste: PVC Schedule 40 above ground, PVC SDR 35 below ground.
   2.3 Storm drainage: PVC Schedule 40, sized per local code.
   2.4 Natural gas: black steel, threaded joints, pressure tested at 30 psi.

3. WATER HEATERS
   3.1 Domestic hot water: 2 gas-fired units, 100-gallon capacity each.
   3.2 Temperature setting: 120 degrees Fahrenheit at fixture.
   3.3 Circulation pump: continuous recirculation, timer-controlled.
