PROJECT: HIGHRISE TOWER - PHASE 2
STRUCTURAL DRAWING SET S-101
TYPICAL FLOOR FRAMING PLAN - LEVELS 5 THROUGH 20

DRAWING NOTES:
1. All dimensions are in feet and inches unless otherwise noted.
2. All concrete shall be normal weight, 145 pcf.
3. Refer to structural specifications for all material requirements.
4. All rebar lap splices per ACI 318-19, Class B.

COLUMN SCHEDULE:
C-1 through C-12: 24" x 24" reinforced concrete columns
- Concrete strength: 5,000 psi (f'c = 5,000 psi) 
- Vertical bars: 12-#11 grade 60
- Tie spacing: 6" o.c.

C-13 through C-20: 30" x 30" reinforced concrete columns  
- Concrete strength: 6,000 psi (f'c = 6,000 psi)
- Vertical bars: 16-#11 grade 60
- Tie spacing: 4" o.c.

BEAM SCHEDULE:
B-1 through B-8: 24" x 18" reinforced concrete beams
- Concrete strength: 5,000 psi (f'c = 5,000 psi)
- Bottom bars: 4-#9 continuous
- Stirrups: #4 at 6" o.c.

B-9 through B-12: 30" x 24" transfer beams
- Concrete strength: 6,000 psi (f'c = 6,000 psi)
- Bottom bars: 6-#11 continuous
- Stirrups: #5 at 4" o.c.

SLAB DETAILS:
Typical elevated slab: 6 inches thick
- Reinforcing: #5 at 12" o.c. both ways
- Top bars at columns per detail A
- Concrete strength: 5,000 psi (f'c = 5,000 psi)

Transfer slab: 14 inches thick
- Reinforcing: #6 at 10" o.c. both ways
- Post-tensioned tendons per PT-001
- Concrete strength: 6,000 psi (f'c = 6,000 psi)

Roof slab: 10 inches thick
- Reinforcing: #5 at 12" o.c. both ways
- Slope 1/4" per foot to drains
- Concrete strength: 5,000 psi (f'c = 5,000 psi)

FOUNDATION:
Mat foundation: 48 inches thick
- Concrete strength: 6,000 psi (f'c = 6,000 psi)
- Bottom reinforcing: #8 at 8" o.c. both ways
- Top reinforcing: #8 at 8" o.c. both ways
- Dowels into columns per detail B

SECTION DETAILS:
Detail A - Column Capital:
- Slab thickens to 10 inches at column head
- Additional top bars: 4-#6 each way
- Dimension from column face: 36 inches all sides

Detail B - Mat Foundation Dowel:
- #11 dowels, 48 inches long
- Standard hooks per ACI 318
- Lap splice with column bars: 36 inches minimum

DETAIL C - BEAM TO COLUMN CONNECTION:
- Beam bottom bars extend 6 inches minimum into column core
- Column ties continue through joint per ACI 318
- Concrete placement sequence: columns first, beams second
