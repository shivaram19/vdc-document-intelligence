# Consultative Outreach Research — Show, Don't Tell

> **Verified sources only.** How to project an idea without selling directly, by demonstrating working solutions that solve the prospect's problem.

---

## 1. The Core Principle: Show, Don't Tell

### Altiorco 2025 — "Touch 3: Show, Don't Tell"
- **Source:** `https://altiorco.com/resources/blog/lead-generation`
- **Finding:** In a 5-touch nurture sequence, Touch 3 is "Show, Don't Tell." Share a short video testimonial from a customer in their industry. "This isn't about features; it's about showcasing a real-world outcome, making the benefits tangible."
- **Application:** Lead with a concrete result, not a feature list.

### Consensus 2025 — Interactive Demo Software
- **Source:** `https://goconsensus.com/blog/shorten-sales-cycles-interactive-demo-tips`
- **Finding:** "This 'show, don't tell' approach helps demystify technical capabilities and builds confidence across both business and technical audiences."
- **Application:** Demo a real working system on their actual documents, not a slide deck.

### Figma 2013 Case Study (Peony.ink 2026)
- **Source:** `https://www.peony.ink/blog/greatest-pitch-decks-analysis`
- **Finding:** Figma's 2013 pre-seed deck had only a few slides — the rest was live demos. "A working prototype proves technical capability more than any architecture diagram."
- **Application:** A live query on their documents beats any product description.

## 2. Jobs-to-Be-Done (JTBD) in Outreach

### Surfe.com 2024 — JTBD Sales Conversations
- **Source:** `https://www.surfe.com/blog/jobs-to-be-done-framework/`
- **Finding:** "Customers don't purchase features. Instead, they 'hire' solutions to problems." Show value in the customer's language:
  - ❌ "Our product enhances team productivity and reduces manual interventions"
  - ✅ "It saves us time and makes the team more efficient"
- **Application:** Frame every message around the outcome they want, not the technology you built.

### Daily.dev — "If You Need to Explain Your Value Prop, You've Already Lost"
- **Source:** `https://business.daily.dev/resources/if-you-need-to-explain-your-value-prop-youve-already-lost/`
- **Finding:** Use JTBD to articulate the "better me." "How will our product make customers' life better from their perspective?"
- **Application:** The message should answer: "What progress will this person make by connecting with me?"

## 3. Consultative Selling & Discovery Questions

### Saber.app 2026 — Discovery Questions
- **Source:** `https://www.saber.app/glossary/discovery-questions`
- **Finding:** Top-performing sales professionals ask 11–14 questions during initial discovery vs 6–8 for average performers. "The quality of discovery questions directly correlates with sales outcomes."
- **Application:** Every outreach message should include ONE open-ended discovery question.

### SPIN Selling (Neil Rackham, 1980s)
- **Source:** `https://zime.ai/blogs/best-sales-methodologies`
- **Study:** 35,000 sales calls analyzed
- **Framework:**
  - **S**ituation: "What tools is your team currently using?"
  - **P**roblem: "Are there situations where visibility is a challenge?"
  - **I**mplication: "What's the consequence when forecasts miss?"
  - **N**eed-payoff: "How valuable would accurate data be?"
- **Result:** Won-vs-lost ratio improved from 10% to 23% after SPIN implementation.
- **Application:** Use Problem + Implication questions in outreach to get prospects to articulate their own pain.

### Lagrowthmachine 2026 — Consultative Selling Flow
- **Source:** `https://lagrowthmachine.com/sales-techniques/`
- **Finding:** Consultative selling flow: Research → Ask & Listen → Teach → Qualify → Close. "Lead with curiosity. Research deeply, ask thoughtful questions, listen more than you talk."
- **Application:** The outreach message should demonstrate research (signal reference) + ask a question (discovery) + offer to teach (share what we learned).

### VelocityAI.io 2026 — Consultative Selling Stats
- **Source:** `https://www.velocityai.io/blog-posts/consultative-selling---definition-methods-and-examples`
- **Finding:** Organizations using consultative selling achieve **49% higher win rate** and **35% larger average deal size** (G2 2024, Qwilr 2024). **37% higher customer retention** (Gartner 2023).
- **Application:** Position as trusted advisor, not vendor.

## 4. Solution Selling: Problem First

### Eubrics 2026 — Solution Selling Case Study
- **Source:** `https://www.eubrics.com/blog/sales-methodology`
- **Finding:** InoVVorX sold AI analytics to manufacturing. Instead of pitching capabilities, they first understood production bottlenecks. "Tailored demos that resonated deeply, increasing conversion rates by over 30%."
- **Application:** Understand their specific Ontario Line challenge first. Then demo the exact solution to that challenge.

### Close.com — Consultative Selling Guide
- **Source:** `https://resource-downloads.close.com/resources/Consultative-Selling-Guide-Close.pdf`
- **Finding:** "Closes by giving real proof of the results. A product demo that proves the value of the product for this particular customer, or offering a free trial period to let the prospect experience the benefits firsthand."
- **Application:** Offer a live demo on their actual document types (tunnel specs, station drawings) — not a generic sandbox.

## 5. The New Outreach Message Formula

Based on all verified sources, the consultative outreach message follows this structure:

```
1. SIGNAL REFERENCE (PostKing 2026: specific reference = 48% acceptance)
   → Reference their job posting, conference talk, or published content

2. VALUE DEMONSTRATION (Altiorco 2025: "show, don't tell")
   → One concrete, specific result from a similar project
   → NOT features. NOT capabilities. A real number, a real outcome.

3. DISCOVERY QUESTION (Saber.app 2026: 11-14 questions = top performers)
   → SPIN-style Problem or Implication question
   → Gets THEM to articulate the pain, not you asserting it

4. LOW-FRICTION CTA (PostKing 2026: "Would love to connect" = 46%)
   → Offer to share what you learned, not to demo your product
   → "Compare notes" > "Schedule a demo"
```
