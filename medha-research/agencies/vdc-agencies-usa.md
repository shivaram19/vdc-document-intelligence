## Cold Outreach Strategy

### Primary Decision Makers
1. VDC Manager / BIM Manager
2. Director of Virtual Design & Construction
3. Director of Preconstruction
4. VP of Operations
5. Project Director

### Outreach Channels (in priority order)
1. **LinkedIn InMail** to VDC/BIM Managers - highest response rate
2. **Cold Email** to info@ or specific VDC department emails
3. **Twitter/X** engagement with VDC content, then DM
4. **Instagram** for brand awareness, less direct sales

### Email Templates

**Template 1: The Contradiction Hook**
Subject: [Company] - Catching drawing-spec mismatches before they hit the field
Body: Hi [Name], I noticed [Company] specializes in VDC/BIM coordination. We built Medha - an AI tool that scans drawings and specs to flag contradictions (dimension mismatches, conflicting requirements) before they become field issues. Would a 5-min demo be worth your time?

**Template 2: The RFI Hook**
Subject: Cutting RFI response time from 4 hours to 30 seconds
Body: Hi [Name], [Company]'s VDC team probably handles dozens of RFIs per project. Medha auto-drafts RFI responses by scanning your entire document set - with full citations. One BIM coordinator saved 12 hours/week. Open to a quick look?

**Template 3: The White-Label Hook**
Subject: White-label AI analytics for your GC clients
Body: Hi [Name], VDC agencies are bundling Medha as "premium AI analytics" and reselling to GC clients for +$1,000/mo. Pure margin. Interested in how [Company] could do the same?
