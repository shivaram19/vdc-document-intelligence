# Competitive Landscape - Construction Document Intelligence

## Direct Competitors (Document AI for Construction)

### Pelles.ai
- **Target:** MEP trade contractors (subcontractors)
- **Features:** AI-powered preconstruction, takeoff automation, document markup, generative AI for MEP designs
- **Strengths:** MEP-specific, path-planning algorithms, cloud-based
- **Weaknesses:** Niche focus (MEP only), not for VDC agencies, no white-label
- **Pricing:** Mid-range, coupon/discount driven

### Constructable
- **Target:** Mid-market GCs ($20M-$200M annual volume)
- **Features:** Unified project management, AI-powered search across project data, RFIs, submittals, drawings, daily logs
- **Strengths:** YC-backed, 500+ projects powered, flat pricing, fast onboarding
- **Weaknesses:** Not white-label, targets GCs not VDC agencies, modular structure
- **Pricing:** Flat monthly subscription (vs Procore's volume-based)

### Procore Copilot / Procore
- **Target:** Enterprise GCs, all construction verticals
- **Features:** Document management, financial tracking, field management, quality/safety
- **Strengths:** Market leader, comprehensive, mobile apps
- **Weaknesses:** Volume-based pricing (six figures for mid-market), modular complexity, not white-label
- **Pricing:** Tied to annual construction volume

### Autodesk Construction Cloud / BIM Collaborate
- **Target:** Design teams, large contractors
- **Features:** Model coordination, Navisworks integration, clash detection, document management
- **Strengths:** Deep BIM integration, industry standard tools
- **Weaknesses:** Requires systems integrator, complex implementation, not AI-native

### Buildots
- **Target:** GCs, project owners
- **Features:** AI-powered construction monitoring, progress tracking
- **Strengths:** Computer vision for site monitoring
- **Weaknesses:** Field-focused, not document intelligence

### Arphie
- **Target:** Preconstruction, bidding
- **Features:** Document analysis for proposals
- **Weaknesses:** Early stage, limited feature set

## Adjacent Competitors

### AutoSpecs (Autodesk)
- Specification management, code compliance
- Faster spec querying, risk mitigation

### Fieldwire (Hilti)
- Field execution, task management, plan markups
- Point solution - no document intelligence

### RedTeam
- Legacy construction management
- Dated interface, infrequent updates
