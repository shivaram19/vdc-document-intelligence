# Medha User Personas - VDC Document Intelligence

## Primary Persona: The Overloaded BIM Coordinator

### Name: Alex Chen
- **Age:** 28-35
- **Role:** BIM / VDC Coordinator
- **Company:** Mid-size GC or VDC agency (50-500 employees)
- **Salary:** $75,000 - $139,000/year
- **Location:** Major metro (NYC, LA, Chicago, Denver, Seattle)

### Daily Pain
- Spends 10-15 hours/week manually reviewing specs, drawings, RFIs
- Ctrl+F through 50+ PDFs to find answers to simple questions
- "What is the concrete strength for columns?" → 45 minutes of searching
- Drafts RFI responses that take 2-4 hours each
- Finds drawing-spec contradictions AFTER they've caused field issues
- Works evenings to catch up on document review

### Tools Used
- Revit (authoring)
- Navisworks (coordination)
- Bluebeam (PDF markup)
- Procore / ACC (document management)
- Excel (tracking)
- SharePoint (file storage)

### Goals
- Find answers in minutes, not hours
- Catch contradictions before they hit the field
- Draft RFIs faster with accurate citations
- Look smart in coordination meetings
- Go home on time

### Quote
"I spent 3 hours yesterday searching for the fire damper spec. It was in the mechanical spec, but I checked the fire protection spec first. Then the architectural notes. Then I had to call the engineer."
