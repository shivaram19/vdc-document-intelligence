PROJECT: DOWNTOWN OFFICE TOWER - STRUCTURAL SPECIFICATIONS
SECTION 03 30 00 - CAST-IN-PLACE CONCRETE

1.1 GENERAL
All concrete work shall comply with ACI 318-19 and local building codes.

1.2 CONCRETE MIX DESIGNS
- Footings: 3,000 psi minimum at 28 days
- Columns: 5,000 psi minimum at 28 days  
- Floor slabs: 4,000 psi minimum at 28 days
- Post-tensioned slabs: 5,000 psi minimum at 28 days, high early strength

1.3 REINFORCING STEEL
All reinforcing shall be ASTM A615 Grade 60.
Standard hook dimensions per ACI 318 Table 25.3.1.

1.4 FIREPROOFING
Structural steel columns and beams shall receive 2-hour fireproofing per IBC Table 601.
Fireproofing material: Spray-applied cementitious, minimum 1.5 inches thick

1.5 STRUCTURAL STEEL
Wide flange beams: ASTM A992, Grade 50
Connection design per AISC 360-16

1.6 DEAD LOADS
Floor finish: 2 psf
Partitions: 15 psf (movable)
Mechanical/electrical: 10 psf
Ceiling: 3 psf

1.7 LIVE LOADS
Office areas: 50 psf
Corridors: 100 psf
Lobby/Atrium: 100 psf
Mechanical rooms: 150 psf
