PROJECT: DOWNTOWN OFFICE TOWER - MECHANICAL SPECIFICATIONS
SECTION 23 00 00 - HEATING, VENTILATING, AND AIR CONDITIONING

1.1 GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
All HVAC systems shall comply with ASHRAE 90.1-2022 energy standards and local building codes.

1.2 TEMPERATURE SETPOINTS
- Office spaces: Heating 70°F, Cooling 74°F (setback 65°F/78°F after hours)
- Conference rooms: Heating 71°F, Cooling 73°F
- Server room: Constant 68°F ± 2°F, 24/7 operation
- Lobby/Atrium: Heating 72°F, Cooling 75°F

1.3 AIR HANDLING UNITS
Provide (4) variable air volume (VAV) air handling units, 25,000 CFM each.
Units shall include:
- MERV 13 filters minimum
- Economizer controls with enthalpy-based switching
- Variable frequency drives (VFD) on all fans
- Discharge air temperature reset: 55°F to 60°F based on zone demand

1.4 DUCTWORK
All ductwork shall be galvanized steel, sealed to SMACNA Seal Class A.
Main trunk velocity: 1,800 FPM maximum
Branch velocity: 1,200 FPM maximum
Minimum duct size: 6 inches diameter

1.5 FIRE PROTECTION COORDINATION
HVAC ducts penetrating fire-rated assemblies shall have fire dampers per NFPA 90A.
Smoke dampers required at all smoke barrier penetrations.
