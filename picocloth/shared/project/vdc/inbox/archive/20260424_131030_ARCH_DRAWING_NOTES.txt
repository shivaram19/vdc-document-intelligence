PROJECT: DOWNTOWN OFFICE TOWER - ARCHITECTURAL DRAWING NOTES
SHEET A-101 - GENERAL NOTES AND BUILDING PARAMETERS

A. OCCUPANCY AND USE
Building occupancy: Group B (Business)
Total building area: 125,000 SF
Number of floors: 8 above grade, 2 below grade (parking)
Floor-to-floor height: 14 feet typical, 18 feet lobby

B. THERMAL AND MOISTURE PROTECTION
Wall R-value: R-21 minimum continuous insulation
Roof R-value: R-30 minimum above deck
Window U-factor: 0.30 maximum
Window SHGC: 0.25 maximum

C. INTERIOR FINISHES
Office flooring: Carpet tile, 24" x 24" modular
Conference room flooring: Luxury vinyl tile (LVT)
Lobby flooring: Polished concrete with stain
Wall finishes: Paint on Level 5 drywall
Ceiling: ACT 2' x 4' tile, 9 feet AFF typical

D. HVAC COORDINATION - ARCHITECTURAL
Mechanical room minimum ceiling height: 12 feet clear
All ceiling plenums shall be minimum 18 inches deep for duct routing
Diffuser locations coordinated with reflected ceiling plan A-201
Thermostat locations per electrical plan E-301

E. FIRE RATED ASSEMBLIES
All shafts: 2-hour rated
Floor assemblies: 2-hour rated
Exit corridors: 1-hour rated
Tenant demising walls: 1-hour rated

F. ACCESSIBILITY
All design shall comply with ADA 2010 Standards and local accessibility codes.
Minimum clear door width: 32 inches
Maximum ramp slope: 1:12
