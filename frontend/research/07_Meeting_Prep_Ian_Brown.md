## STRUCTURED QUESTIONS BY CATEGORY

### A. HIS WORK & EXPERTISE (First 10 minutes — build rapport)
1. "What exactly are you building right now — is it a product, a research project, or a company?"
2. "How did you get into construction robotics specifically? Most 21-year-olds are building chatbots."
3. "What's your technical stack — are you working on the data pipeline side, the model side, or the robotics hardware side?"
4. "Are you affiliated with a university lab, YC, or bootstrapping solo?"

### B. DATA COLLECTION (The core topic)
5. "When you say 'construction robotics data,' what modalities are you actually working with — visual, LiDAR, force-torque, BIM overlays?"
6. "Are you seeing companies collect data continuously on-site, or is it still mostly mission-based scanning?"
7. "What's the most common data format you're encountering — ROS bags, HDF5, point clouds, or something proprietary?"
8. "Who physically operates the data collection — the GC's team, the robotics vendor, or a third-party scanning agency?"
9. "How do people handle the frequency mismatch — design data is static, robot data is 100Hz real-time, site surveys are weekly?"

### C. TRAINING & ANNOTATION (Where Trelo Labs can add value)
10. "How are construction robotics companies handling annotation? Teleoperation, human demonstration, simulation, or self-supervised?"
11. "What's the biggest bottleneck you've seen in going from raw site data to a trainable dataset?"
12. "Are companies using foundation models and VLA architectures, or still training task-specific models per robot?"
13. "How do they handle the sim-to-real gap? Construction environments are unstructured — weather, dust, changing layouts."
14. "Do you see anyone doing auto-tagging or auto-annotation from BIM-to-as-built comparison?"

### D. EVALUATION & DEPLOYMENT
15. "What does 'evaluation' look like in construction robotics — success rate, safety incidents, task completion time, deviation from BIM?"
16. "Are robots deployed in closed prefab environments or open construction sites?"
17. "How is data fed back after deployment — continuous learning or static models that need retraining?"

### E. SUSTAINABILITY & ESG (Differentiator angle)
18. "Is anyone capturing sustainability data — embodied carbon, material waste, energy use — through robotics systems?"
19. "LEED v5 made embodied carbon a prerequisite in 2025. Do you see robotics data being used for ESG reporting?"
20. "Where do you think the intersection of robotics data and digital twins is heading for urban architecture?"

### F. MARKET & PARTNERSHIPS (Closing — assess fit)
21. "Where do you think the 'GPT moment' for robotics actually is — is it the model architecture, the data aggregation, or the simulation fidelity?"
22. "Who should we be talking to in your network — robotics companies, general contractors, or the agencies that serve them?"
23. "If Trelo Labs wanted to become the data infrastructure layer for construction robotics, what would we need to build first to be credible?"
24. "Are you looking for partners, funding, or customers — what's your biggest need right now?"

**Angle 1: Data Infrastructure Gap**
