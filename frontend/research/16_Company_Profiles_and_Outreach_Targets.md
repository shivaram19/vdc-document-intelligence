## PROFILE DEEP DIVES

### Buildots (Tel Aviv / London / Philadelphia)
- **Founded:** 2018
- **Funding:** [$121M total (Intel Capital, TLV Partners, Lightspeed, etc.)](https://buildots.com/blog/buildots-secures-15m-intel-capital-led-investment/)
- **Product:** Hardhat-mounted 360° cameras + computer vision; automated progress tracking vs. BIM schedule
- **Key Differentiator:** Delay Forecast AI; integrates with BIM 360
- **Tech Stack:** CV, 3D reconstruction, BIM comparison
- **What's Missing:** No LLM layer. Progress data is visual/dashboard-only. Executives can't ask "why is floor 3 delayed?" in natural language.
- **Trayini.ai Angle:** Add LLM orchestration on top of Buildots data. Natural language queries, automated executive summaries, RFI generation from deviations.
- **Person to Contact:** VP Product or Head of Partnerships
- **Message:** "Your 7B sq ft of progress data is a goldmine. We can add a natural language interface so project executives ask questions instead of reading dashboards."

### OpenSpace (California / UK / Australia)
- **Founded:** 2017
- **Funding:** [$102M Series D, $902M valuation](https://www.openspace.ai/press-releases/openspace-extends-leadership-with-102m-series-d-financing/)

- **Key Differentiator:** [7B+ sq ft captured; fastest processing in industry](https://www.openspace.ai/blog/openspace-2025-review/)
- **Tech Stack:** Photogrammetry, AI alignment, BIM comparison
- **What's Missing:** No semantic LLM interface. Users browse photos; they can't query "show me all unresolved clashes on level 5."
- **Trayini.ai Angle:** LLM interface for OpenSpace captures. Natural language queries across 7B sq ft of visual data. "Find all instances where MEP routing conflicts with structural."
- **Person to Contact:** Head of Partnerships or VP Product
- **Message:** "7B sq ft captured but no brain on top. We can make your visual data queryable in plain English."
