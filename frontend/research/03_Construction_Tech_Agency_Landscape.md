# CONSTRUCTION TECH AGENCY LANDSCAPE REPORT
## Prepared for Trayini.ai | April 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The construction technology agency landscape is bifurcated between **legacy service providers** (BIM/CAD outsourcers, Autodesk resellers, VDC consultants) and **AI-native startups** (computer vision, robotics, LLM-based field tools). The market is highly fragmented with significant gaps in AI/robotics implementation, creating a clear partnership and white-label opportunity for Trayini.ai. Construction firms in 2025-2026 are prioritizing efficiency software, AI-powered preconstruction, digital twins, and field automation — capabilities that legacy agencies struggle to deliver.

## 2. LEGACY AGENCY SERVICES

### Core Service Categories
1. **BIM Modeling & Coordination** — Architectural, structural, MEP modeling; clash detection; 4D/5D BIM
2. **CAD Drafting & Conversion** — 2D drafting, PDF-to-CAD, CAD-to-BIM, shop drawings
3. **Scan-to-BIM / Reality Capture** — Laser scanning, point cloud processing, as-built documentation
4. **Software Implementation & Training** — Autodesk/Procore/Navisworks deployment, template creation, staff training
5. **Staff Augmentation / Dedicated Teams** — Offshore BIM modelers, drafters, VDC coordinators working as client extensions
6. **IT Support & CDE Setup** — Common Data Environment configuration, BIM 360/ACC administration
7. **VDC Management** — Model health, coordination meetings, federated model management

### Typical Pricing Models
| Model | Rate Range (Offshore India) | Rate Range (Onshore US) |
|-------|---------------------------|------------------------|
| Hourly (BIM modeler/drafter) | $15–$35/hour | $65–$150/hour |

| Project-based fixed fee | $5,000–$50,000+ | $25,000–$500,000+ |
| Software implementation | — | $10,000–$100,000+ |
