PROJECT: DOWNTOWN OFFICE TOWER - FIRE PROTECTION SPECIFICATIONS
SECTION 21 13 13 - WET PIPE SPRINKLER SYSTEMS

1.1 GENERAL
All fire protection systems shall comply with NFPA 13 (2022 Edition) and local AHJ requirements.

1.2 SPRINKLER DESIGN CRITERIA
- Office areas: Light hazard, design density 0.10 gpm/SF over 1,500 SF
- Mechanical rooms: Ordinary Hazard Group 1, design density 0.15 gpm/SF
- Parking garages: Ordinary Hazard Group 1, design density 0.15 gpm/SF
- Storage areas: Ordinary Hazard Group 2, design density 0.20 gpm/SF

1.3 SPRINKLER SPACING
Maximum spacing between sprinklers: 15 feet on center
Maximum spacing from walls: 7.5 feet
Maximum coverage per sprinkler: 225 SF (light hazard)

1.4 WATER SUPPLY
Fire pump required: 1,000 GPM at 120 psi
Water supply test within 12 months required prior to system acceptance.

1.5 STANDPIPES
Class I standpipe system required per IBC 905.3
Standpipe outlet spacing: Every floor, within 150 feet of any point

1.6 FIRE ALARM
Addressable fire alarm system per NFPA 72
Smoke detectors in all return air plenums per NFPA 90A
Duct smoke detectors at all air handling units
Manual pull stations at all exits
