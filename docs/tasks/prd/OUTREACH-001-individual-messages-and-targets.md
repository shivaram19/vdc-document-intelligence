## If They Ask "What Are You Building?"

**Deflection script (use on the call, not in the message):**
> "I'm still in learning mode — first I need to understand how people actually work today. If I find a pattern across enough conversations, I might explore a solution. But right now I'm just trying to understand your world. There are no right or wrong answers."

**Never say:**
- "We're building an AI platform..."
- "I have this idea for..."
- "Would you use a tool that..."

**Always say:**
- "I'm conducting a study on..."
- "I'm trying to understand how..."
- "Tell me about the last time..."

## Quick Reminders

1. **Lead with THEIR world, not yours.** Don't open with "IIT Madras graduate researching..." — open with why their specific experience matters.
2. **Explicitly say "no pitch."** Removes the primary objection.
3. **Offer value in return.** "Happy to share anonymized findings afterward."
4. **Use academic language.** "Study," "research," "understand" — not "validate," "pitch," "demo."
5. **If no response in 5 days, send the follow-up message** from the templates above.
