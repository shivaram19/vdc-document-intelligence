## Core Hypothesis

An agentic enterprise is not a stack of tools. It is an operating model where **goal-directed agents perform work, use tools, and are accountable through observability**, with humans retaining authority over values, commitments, and exceptions. For Medha, the minimum viable form is **one founder + one orchestrator + specialist agents per pipeline**, with human approval gates.
