# Q8: Where Are the Communities Building Agentic Enterprises?

**Research Question:** Which communities, groups, and movements are aligned with building human-centered, open-source agentic enterprises — and how can Medha learn from and contribute to them?

---

## Why This Matters for Medha

No one builds this alone. Communities provide talent, feedback, partnerships, and moral direction. We need to know where the right people gather.

---

## Sub-Questions

### 8.1 Technical Communities
1. Where do MCP/A2A developers gather? (Discord, Slack, GitHub Discussions, forums)
2. Where do n8n power users and contributors gather?
3. Where do LangChain/LangGraph developers share production patterns?
4. Are there communities specifically for open-source agent infrastructure?

### 8.2 Founder Communities
1. Where do solo founders building AI-native companies gather?
2. Which communities focus on AI-augmented operations rather than AI products?
3. Where are construction-tech founders active?
4. Where are India/GCC startup founders active?

### 8.3 Human-Centered AI Communities
1. Which groups focus on responsible AI, AI ethics, and human-AI collaboration?
2. Are there communities exploring AI labor, dignity, and ownership?
3. Which organizations promote open-source AI governance?
4. Where do critics of unchecked AI autonomy gather?

### 8.4 Open-Source Construction Communities
1. Are there open-source communities around BIM, IFC, OpenCDE, BCF?
2. Where do OpenBIM and buildingSMART communities interact?
3. Are there construction-tech open-source collectives?
4. Where can Medha share its research and tools?

### 8.5 Contribution Strategy
1. What can Medha contribute to these communities? (code, research, documentation, case studies)
2. What can Medha learn from them?
3. Who are potential early collaborators or advisors?
4. How does community participation fit Medha's business goals?

---

## Current Hypotheses

1. The most relevant technical communities are around **MCP, n8n, and LangChain**.
2. The most relevant founder communities are **Indie Hackers, Build in Public, and construction-tech LinkedIn**.
3. The most relevant human-centered AI communities are **Mozilla, AI Now, and Center for Humane Technology**.
4. Medha's best contribution is **documented, real-world case studies** of building a minimally agentic enterprise.
